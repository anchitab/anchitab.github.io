Hello! I'm Anchita Bora

    I'm an aspiring software engineer/product manager studying computer science in sunny southern California. I'm currently an undergdaruate student at UC Riverside's, Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering, and am a first-year CS student.
    My computer science journey began in the 7th grade when I was selected as one of the 20 girls for a summer program by GE x UC Berkeley Engineering. During this program, I learned how to program on Scratch and drafted my first app idea. It was after this exposure, I knew I wanted to learn more! In high school, I continiued my interest and took all the computer science courses my school offered and got involved in local startups working on web development. Now, I'm a univeristy student studying one of the coolest fields on this planet!

    Let's talk tech. In high school, I played around with HTML, CSS, and some MongoDB and built a website for Stanford's Pre-Collegiate Program's Counselors to help communicate with students efficiently. In college, I dove into object-oriented programming and have created projects in labs including a Bulletin Board and Archer Game with C++. I am currently expanding my programming toolbox and am learning Python, Matlab, and SQl.

    I'm passionate about diversity in tech, especially women empowerment in this field. It simply doesn't make sense that females make up more than half of the consumers of tech products yet are barely a fraction of the teams making them. I am glad to be a part of such welcmong communties like Rewriting the Code and WinC. Furthermore, I believe that everyone should have equal access to computer programmng which is why I helped create curriculum for the Youth Competitve Programming Circle.

    In my free time, you can find me at my local spin studio, eating good food, or traveling!
