I'm an aspiring software engineer/product manager studying computer science in sunny southern California @UC Riverside.

My tech journey started in high school at a Stanford summer program where I was introduced to web dev, and since then, I have continued to learn new technologies, languages, and frameworks and am excited to expand on my current knowledge!
